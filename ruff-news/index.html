Planning, creating, and strategizing

When I began Ruff News, I made one clear goal: create everything from scratch. This way, I would grow my skillset and gain experience launching a content/blog site I built myself. I built the site in pure HTML and CSS at first before migrating it to WordPress. I learned and developed my skills in SCSS, jQuery, semantic HTML, grid systems, content writing, graphic design, and more. I continue to learn and contribute to the site on a daily basis, trying out new features and expanding the functionality.
